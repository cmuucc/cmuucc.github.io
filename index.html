A word from our Executive Board

Welcome! We are excited to tell you about us.

Our student-run consulting organization based in the University of Michigan specializes in providing quality pro-bono consulting services to local businesses, startups, and nonprofits each semester, with diverse projects.

Looking back at how far we have grown, we are proud of the progress that we have made and we attribute this success to the dedicated members in our club and our strong relationships with our clients. We hope to continue our legacy as the fastest growing consulting club on campus and we look forward to getting to know you.

Sincerely,
APEX Consulting Group Executive Board.

So who are we and what do we do?

APEX Consulting Group is a student-run organization at the University of Michigan. At APEX, we provide quality pro-bono consulting services for local businesses, startups, and nonprofits each semester. We pride ourselves for our entrepreneurial approach when addressing our client's needs and the impact that we make by exceeding expectations and helping them grow.

We also work towards enriching the experiences of individuals at the University of Michigan through these hands-on consulting projects, equipping them with the skills and experiences necessary to grow into strategic leaders with the ability to solve complex problems in the business world.
